About Us

Our Story & Mission

Who We Are

Sky High Running Company is an Indianapolis-based trail running race company built on the belief that running should do more than test your limits — it should give back to the places we run and the people around us.

Founded by a group of passionate trail runners, we create race experiences that combine the thrill of endurance running with meaningful conservation and community impact.

Our Philosophy

↑

Explore

We design courses that take runners through stunning, often overlooked natural landscapes. Every race is a chance to discover something new about the trail and yourself.

♥

Giveback

A portion of every registration goes to trail maintenance and local conservation nonprofits. We also organize volunteer trail days throughout the year.

☆

Thrive

Whether you're running your first 5K or your tenth ultra, our races are designed to help you grow as a runner and connect with a supportive community.

Sustainable Running

We're committed to leaving trails better than we found them. Our races use minimal single-use materials, partner with local conservation groups, and prioritize environmental stewardship at every level.

From compostable cups at aid stations to post-race trail cleanups, sustainability isn't an afterthought — it's built into everything we do.

Join the Movement

Run with purpose. Explore new trails. Give back to your community.

Glacier

Endurance Trail Race

The Race

Glacier is Sky High Running's flagship endurance race — a demanding course through glacial terrain, dense forest, and rugged singletrack. Designed for runners who want to test their limits in the heart of Indiana's most challenging trails.

With distance options ranging from 10K to 50 miles, Glacier welcomes committed trail runners at every level. Whether you're chasing a PR or simply chasing the finish line, this race will push you.

Course Highlights

The Glacier course winds through some of Indiana's most scenic and technical trail systems. Expect rolling hills, creek crossings, dense canopy sections, and open meadow stretches.

All courses are professionally marked and measured. Detailed course maps and elevation profiles will be available to registered runners before race day.

Arcadia

Scenic Trail Race

The Race

Arcadia is Sky High Running's scenic trail race — a beautiful course through rolling hills, creek crossings, wildflower meadows, and shaded forest paths. It's the perfect race for trail runners of all levels.

With distances from 5K to marathon, Arcadia is ideal whether you're new to trail running or a seasoned veteran looking for a fast, fun course through Indiana's hidden natural beauty.

Course Highlights

The Arcadia course showcases some of Indiana's most beautiful and accessible trail systems. The terrain is forgiving but engaging, with gentle elevation changes and stunning natural scenery throughout.

All courses are professionally marked. Post-race festivities include local food vendors, live music, and awards for all age groups.
